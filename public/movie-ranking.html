Best Recent Animes According to Wilson

This list will consist of animes from the last 2ish years

Hell's Paradise

This show has awesome battles, dynamic characters, and really invokes a lot of emotions in my heart. Also, I like how its not too childish as it sometimes has mature romance which people are fighting for their lives. The world building is so good and makes me want to live in the world as well. I love the power system and how the protagonists are slowly discovering it to defeat the bad buys.

Blue Lock

Its not that deep but god damn it got me into soccer. Great animations, story lines, and characteres!

Haikyuu

Just a banger. Nothing better than young kids giving it all to chase their dreams.
